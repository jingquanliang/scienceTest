Censys Login

Censys is freely available to researchers. However we require users to create an account and adhere to our Terms of Service to prevent abuse.

If you find yourself restricted by the default rate limit or search syntax, please contact us. We will happily provide authenticated researchers with full SQL access and less restrictive API limits.
